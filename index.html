POMODORO

Pomodoro Clock

The pomodoro technique is a time management method that uses a timer to break down work into intervals separated by short breaks.

By default, this app is set to a 25 minutes work session and a 5 minute break session.

You can adjust the work and break sessions by toggling the plus and minus buttons next to the respective session length.

When a session has completed, a short ding will sound and you'll switch to the next session type.
